BABYPASA — DEVELOPER NOTE
E11 Out for Delivery Email
====================================================================

TRIGGER
-------
Webhook status : "Out for Delivery"
State transition: IN_TRANSIT → OUT_FOR_DELIVERY
Meta update     : update_post_meta( $order_id, '_upaya_email_state', 'OUT_FOR_DELIVERY' )

SEND TIMING
-----------
Send immediately when webhook fires. This is the most time-sensitive
email in the sequence — the customer needs to know NOW so they can
be available. Do not batch or delay.

DIFFERENCE FROM E07
-------------------
Duplicate E07 and apply these changes only:

1. Hero icon   : bike/scooter (SVG) instead of truck
2. Hero copy   : "Your order is out for delivery!" /
                 "Get ready to receive your little one's essentials!"
3. Tracker     : Steps 1,2,3,4 → completed (dark pink)
                 Step 4 "Out for delivery" → ACTIVE with "Now" badge
                 Connectors 1→2, 2→3, 3→4 → #ec4899 (completed)
                 Connector 4→5 → #fbcfe8 (upcoming)
                 Step 5 → upcoming (white circle, #fbcfe8 border)
4. Phone banner: Replace tracking code sub-section with pink
                 "Keep your phone handy!" banner (see below)
5. Items header: "What's being delivered today" (not "on its way")
6. Delivery tile: "Delivering to you today with care."

PHONE BANNER COPY (positive tone — no mention of returns)
----------------------------------------------------------
Title : Keep your phone handy!
Body  : Our delivery partner will call or SMS you just before
        they arrive so you don't miss a thing.
Style : background:#fce7f3; border:1px solid #fbcfe8;
        Left icon circle: background:#ec4899, icon: phone-call SVG
        Title color: #9d174d
        Body color : #be185d

TRACKER STYLES QUICK REFERENCE (E11)
--------------------------------------
Step 1 (Order Placed)  : background:#ec4899  ← completed
Step 2 (Picked Up)     : background:#ec4899  ← completed
Step 3 (In Transit)    : background:#ec4899  ← completed
Step 4 (Out Delivery)  : background:#ec4899 + border:3px solid #9d174d ← ACTIVE
Step 5 (Delivered)     : background:#ffffff; border:2px solid #fbcfe8  ← upcoming

Connector 1→2 : background:#ec4899  ← completed
Connector 2→3 : background:#ec4899  ← completed
Connector 3→4 : background:#ec4899  ← completed
Connector 4→5 : background:#fbcfe8  ← upcoming

SUBJECT LINE
------------
Your order #{{order_number}} is out for delivery today! 🛵

PLACEHOLDERS USED
-----------------
{{first_name}}       $order->get_billing_first_name()
{{order_number}}     $order->get_order_number()
{{tracking_code}}    Webhook payload
{{payment_method}}   $order->get_payment_method_title()
{{product_name}}     Loop: $item->get_name()
{{product_qty}}      Loop: $item->get_quantity()

Payment badge: if ( $order->is_paid() ) → Paid / else → Unpaid

TONE NOTE
---------
Keep all copy positive and warm — no mention of returns, missed
deliveries, or negative consequences. The phone banner achieves
the same practical goal (customer stays available) with excitement
rather than anxiety.
